### E-mail Configuration

To enable E-mail for address verification, go to Rocket.Chat Administration panel under Settings -> E-mail -> SMTP and set as follow:

- Protocole: `smtp`
- Host: `localhost`
- Port: `25`
- From -mail: `rocketchat@domain.tld`
